PROJECT
SHOWCASE

Gauthier is a sports and lifestyle photographer that wanted to display his vast array of pictures in a more convenient and easy way than on social media. We both agreed we wanted the website to have a classy and yet edgy feel. More importantly we wanted the website to be free of loading times and to allow the user to move through series of pictures effortlessly. I suggested to develop the website with React, a framework offering SPA websites. Thus giving the user a seamless experience whilst browsing through collections of photographs.
